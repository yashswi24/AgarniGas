Retail Outlet Process

1
Meeting for CBG Retail Outlet Business
This step involves organizing a meeting to discuss the CBG Retail Outlet business.
2
Land Verification & Check Feasibility
Verify the land and check its feasibility for the Retail Outlet project.
3
Issue LOI with Retail Outlet Drawings
Provide a Letter of Intent (LOI) along with Retail Outlet drawings to proceed with the project.
4
NOC of Retail Outlet from Govt. Department
Obtain No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the relevant Government Department for the Retail Outlet.
5
Construction of Retail Outlet
Initiate the construction process for the Retail Outlet.
6
Installation of Electricity Connection
Arrange for the installation of electricity connection for the Retail Outlet.
7
Installation of Machinery & Equipment
Install the necessary machinery and equipment for the Retail Outlet.
8
Installation of Signage and Guidelines
Install signage and provide guidelines for the Retail Outlet.
9
Testing and Start Retail Outlet for Sale of CBG.
Conduct tests to ensure that the machinery and equipment are functioning properly.
Begin operations for the Retail Outlet to sell Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG).
